Reports

expand_more

The sales report shows the number of sales and profit for each menu item between a provided time window. Sales are sorted from most sold to least sold.


The excess report displays all items that have only sold less than 10% of their inventory between the timestamp and today.


The restock report displays all of the current products whose current inventory is less than the minimum specified threshold, meaning that it is in a critical state and needs to be restocked.

The sells together report displays the top 10 pairs of items that are commonly sold together, accounting for general popularity bias.
